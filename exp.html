Weapons

sunder C

A sizable claymore meant to match the height of its wielder, this blade has escorted Belial since his first encounter with the divine. It has long since lost its divine glow, however it continues to loyally serve the oathbreaker.

galeforce C

This weapon has been enchanted with the soul of an air elemental. The blade looks like a gust of wind, and when in use, it howls like a gale, as if enjoying the bite of flesh. When you crit on an attack, anybody (friend or foe) adjacent to you must roll a DC 13 Wisdom save or be frightened by the air elemental trapped inside of this weapon. After each crit, the wind that's bound to the blade fades a little as if the magic is sapped, and after six crits, it turns into a normal rank C melee weapon.
slashing frightened 6/6

vilebite C

this weapon is a noxious green splattered with blood. on your first three attacks per combat encounter, your target must succeed on a DC13 CON saving throw or be POISONED for three rounds. while equipped, PERCEPTION and SURVIVAL checks to find or track you relying on smell are done at advantage.
slashing poisoned

Trinkets

emergency bandaid E

When this ITEM is worn, it will automatically remove the bleeding condition from the wearer at the beginning of their turn. This has two uses before it breaks.
bleed dispel 2/2

a gift from a friend E

A diamond-shaped locket with an unassuming appearance for its exterior; faded, small red stones lining a black pattern on the surface, coated in silver to match the chain it dangles from. Inside, safely secured with an enchantment, are the petals of a White Lily, magic gently embedded into them to protect its wearer from whatever harm would befall them. When you use a defensive technique to mitigate damage, roll a secondary defensive technique and add that dice (min 1d10) to the total mitigation.
damage mitigation

glass cannon D

This JEWELRY ACCESSORY allows you to add +2 damage when trying to do melee attacks. Fragile. Breaks after use in 1 thread.
damage bonus broken

dead man's coin D

A rusted coin on a piece of twine that can be worn like a necklace. Sacrifice 2d10 HP to summon a Rank D skeleton pirate to fight for you. This skeleton operates the same way that a companion does. You must use your bonus action to instruct them to act. This trinket can be used once per encounter.
rank d summon

spiteful band B

This accessory can be activated to put the fear of the gods you definitely don't believe in into your enemies. Once per encounter, you may use your bonus action to make a target within range make a DEX saving throw of 14. If they fail; their armor is Sundered, and they are Fettered for one round.
dc 14 dex sundered fettered

golden rabbit's foot B

The luckiest rabbit's foot ever. Two times per thread, when rolling a STR skill check you may treat a d20 roll of a 9 or lower as a 10. In addition, your critical threat range is increased by 1.
crit range +1 str

void ring B

While wearing this jewelry access and rolling a strength check, treat a d20 roll of 9 or lower as a 10. Additionally, this accessory increases the DC of your Embargoed and Paranoid conditions by 4.
embaroed/paranoid dc +4 str

hat of unnecessarily sultry costumes A

When a creatures places this hat on their head, they automatically become disguised, giving them the appearance of a highly attractive member of a randomly determined race wearing an overly and somewhat arbitrarily skimpy version of a costume randomly rolled using this table. The hat cannot be removed unless a greater dispel is cast. There is a 50% chance the illusion will be of the opposite sex. A creature can use their main action to inspect your appearance and must succeed a DC 15 Intelligence (Investigation) check to see through the spell. Lasts 1d4 hours before the magic fades. Has 5 uses before the magic fades.

gauntlets of ogre power SSS

While wearing these gauntlets, you have advantage on strength checks to push, pull, drag, or lift objects that you normally would be unable to move. While in combat, you have advantage on grapple checks.
str adv
